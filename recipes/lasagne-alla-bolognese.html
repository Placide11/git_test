Lasagne alla Bolognese Recipe

Lasagne alla Bolognese is a classic Italian dish that combines layers of rich Bolognese sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, and lasagne noodles. This hearty and comforting dish is perfect for a special occasion or a family gathering. The slow-cooked Bolognese sauce, made with ground meat, tomatoes, and aromatic herbs, adds a delicious depth of flavor to the dish.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagne noodles
  • For the Bolognese sauce:
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the béchamel sauce:
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Salt and nutmeg to taste
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, carrot, and celery. Sauté until the vegetables are soft and fragrant.
  2. Add the ground beef to the pan and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  3. If using, pour in the red wine and let it simmer until most of the liquid evaporates.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let the sauce simmer for at least 1 hour to develop the flavors.
  5. In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and cook for a minute, stirring constantly.
  6. Gradually whisk in the milk, ensuring there are no lumps. Cook the sauce until it thickens, then season with salt and nutmeg.
  7. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  8. Cook the lasagne noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  9. Assemble the lasagne by spreading a layer of Bolognese sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Top with a layer of lasagne noodles, followed by a layer of béchamel sauce. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of béchamel sauce on top.
  10. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the top of the lasagne.
  11. Bake in the preheated oven for about 30-35 minutes, or until the lasagne is bubbling and golden brown on top.
  12. Remove from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
  13. Slice the lasagne into portions and serve hot. Enjoy!
